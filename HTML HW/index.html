Liverpool Football Club

Liverpool Football Club is a professional football club based in Liverpool, England.
The club competes in the Premier League, the top tier of English football.
Founded in 1892, the club joined the Football League the following year and has played
its home games at Anfield since its formation.

Head coach: Yurgen Klopp
Stadium: Anfield Road
Founded: 1892
